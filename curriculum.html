Our College has a reputation for its vocational courses. The challenege of the new millennium is to equip our school leavers to enter a variety of post-school employment, education and training options. The goal of these courses is to develop students that are multi-skilled and able to transfer knowledge, skills and competencies through vocationally oriented courses to the workplace. The College also offers a number of TAFE courses to extend the curriculum. These courses include:

  • Information Technology
  • Metals and Engineering
  • Retail
  • Construction
  • Business Services
  • TAFE Based Vocational Courses

At the College, we encourage our students to explore their individual gifts and talents by offering a variety of initiatives to support and provide opportunities to develop into committed, independent learners.
