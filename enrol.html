Enrolment

Patrician Brothers challenges all students to achieve their personal best. The College has flourished over a period of 58 years with a strong spiritual, academic and sporting tradition, developing an impressive list of notable ex-students who have excelled in a variety of arenas. Our academic and sporting success has sealed our present strong reputation, and has been extended into areas of Information Technology, Creative and Performing Arts, Debating, Public Speaking and Vocational Education.

Open evening - Patrician Brothers' College Fairfield is a Years 7-12 Catholic Boys school striving for academic excellence in the Patrician Tradition. The College is a culturally diverse community of staff, students and parents working together to provide a sound education catering for the needs of the whole person, a safe environment, a sense of belonging, and a commitment to the values of the Gospel and the Patrician spirit.

Required Documents Please provide copies of the following:

  • school reports with academic records for (at least) the previous year
  • some form of identification, such as a birth certificate
  • passport, and associated visas, if coming from overseas.
  • medical record, especially if there has been any special medical condition such as physical or mental disability
  • endorsement from your parish priest or parish of worship
  • Last NAPLAN results
  • any other documents that supports the "Expression of Interest to enroll"
  • A previous bill (preferably land rates) to certify current residential address
