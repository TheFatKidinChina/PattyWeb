Message from School Principal
Peter Wade

Welcome to the website of Patrician Brothers' College, Fairfield. This website will provide you with a wealth of information about the College, and help you understand the strong tradition of providing a Patrician Catholic education to the boys of the Fairfield district.

The website has been designed not only to provide you with information, but acts as a means of communication and keeping in touch with present and past parents, staff and students. The site will hopefully be used as a learning tool for students, helping them keep in touch with teachers and provide important educational links to other sites of learning. I encourage vistors to this website to view the weekly college newsletter; "The Link" which encapsulates our wonderful, vibrant community.

The College has flourished over a period of 58 years with a strong spiritual, academic and sporting tradition, developing an impressive list of notable ex-students who have excelled in a variety of arenas. Our academic and sporting success has sealed our present strong reputation, and has been extended into areas of Information Technology, Creative and Performing Arts, Debating, Public Speaking and Vocational Education. My hope is that this website will sew another thread into a tapestry that has already been tightly woven by the Patrician Brothers, Parents, Students and Staff, people who have contributed so significantly in making Patrician Brothers' College, Fairfield a special place of spirituality, learning, hospitality and welcome. I look forward to you visiting our website regularly and providing us with constructive comments on its design and content.

With my best wishes and God's blessing,
